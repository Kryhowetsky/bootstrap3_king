The national hero of Ukraine

King Danulo Galician the ruler of the Galician-Volyn principality

Here's a time line of King Danulo Galicin's life:

  • 1201 - Born in Galych
  • 1211 - The nobles planted Danyla to reign in Galich
  • 1215 - With the support of the prince of Cracow, Leszka I White, Danylo became a prince in Volodymyr-Volynskyi
  • 1221 - Mstislav with the help of Danylo expelled the Hungarians from Galich
  • 1223 - Danylo together with other Kyiv-Rus princes participated in the battle of Kalka against the Mongols. He was wounded in his chest and retreated from the battlefield.
  • 1233 - Hungarian troops were defeated under Shumsk. Before the battle Danilo Romanovich prayed in the city church of St. Simeon
  • 1238 - Danilo Romanovich turned himself the Galician throne. To the title of Volyn prince added the title of Prince Galitsky
  • 1239 - Danylo took Kyiv, where there was no prince, planted there his viceroy - voivod Dmitry.
  • 1245 - In the battle of Yaroslav, the army of Danylo and his brother Vasilko defeated the regiments of Prince Rostislav of Chernigov, the Galician boyars, Hungarians and Poles, who completed the almost 40-year struggle for power over the Galician-Volyn principality. This battle was one of the greatest in the history of Rus' of the XIII century.
  • 1253 - In the city of Dorogochina in December 1253 (January 1254), the papal legate crowned and anointed Daniel Romanovich, who received a royal decoration "from all his bishops," emphasizing that he was crowned (which at the same time was the open call of the Golden Orde, because as the vassal of Khan Danylo Romanovich did not had the right to do this ) not only he but also Rus.
  • 1257 - King Danylo Galician convinced of the inability of Rome to organize a crusade against the Golden Horde, in 1257, ceased to have a relationship with the Pope, by the forces of his own struggled against the Hordeans.
  • 1255 - The troops of the king freed the troops from the Kuremsy land along the Southern Bug, Sluchi and Tetereva, took Vozv'iagel'. Danylo managed to defend the Tatars of Bakota (Podillya) and return invaidered cities in Volhynia.
  • 1264 - The king Danylo Galician became ill and died in the Holm his capitol city, where he was buried in the Church of the Holy Mother of God, built for his life. The chronicler, mourning for his death, called him "the second according to Solomon."

"This prince was a good prince, brave and wise; built many cities and temples and removed them with different colors. Most of all, he became famous for his brothers Vasilkov. He was the second in Solomon."
